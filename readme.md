# ✈️ Global Aviation Performance Analytics Dashboard

> End-to-End Data Analytics Project using **Microsoft Excel, MySQL, and Power BI**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Global Aviation Performance Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Excel, MySQL, and Power BI**.

The objective of this project is to analyze the global aviation industry from **2010 to 2026** by evaluating airline financial performance, passenger traffic, fleet expansion, route performance, and aviation safety. The project transforms raw datasets into meaningful business insights through SQL analysis and interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze airline financial performance
- Monitor passenger traffic trends
- Compare Airbus and Boeing aircraft orders
- Evaluate fleet growth and market share
- Identify high-performing airline routes
- Analyze aviation incidents and safety trends
- Support data-driven business decisions

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- North America generated the highest airline revenue.
- Passenger traffic declined significantly during COVID-19 before recovering.
- Airbus received the highest number of aircraft orders.
- Long-haul routes generated higher average ticket fares.
- Aviation incidents remained relatively low compared to total passenger traffic.
- Fleet expansion showed a positive relationship with airline revenue.
